This is a short course at the Pacific Causal Inference Conference ([PCIC 2026](https://spco.cc/pcic2026/))
held at Nankai University, Tianjin, China.
This course targets graduate students, undergraduate students, clinicians, and statisticians in industry.
This course is taught by Xiao-Hua Andrew Zhou, Yuhao Deng, and Chunyuan Zheng.


Outline: <br />
Randomized Experiments; <br />
Observational Studies: Ignorability; <br />
Causal Graphs; <br />
Mediation Analysis; <br />
Principal Stratification; <br />
Application of Causal Inference in Medical Research: Intercurrent Events; <br />
Observational Studies: Non-Ignorability; <br />
Attribution; <br />
Deep Learning for Causal Effect Estimation in Complex Scenarios; <br />
Applications of Causal Methods in Real-World Scenarios; <br />
